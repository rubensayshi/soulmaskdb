# Seed Sources

How to obtain each of the 21 plantable seeds in Soulmask. This data is community-sourced (Reddit, Steam guides, wikis) — not extractable from game files since seeds come from harvesting world plants and looting containers.

Structured data lives in `Game/Parsed/seed_sources.json`.

## Acquisition Methods

| Method            | Description                                                                 |
|-------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Grinder**       | Process harvested crop → seed. Requires "Seed Selection" tech (Mask Lv10). |
| **Barracks Farm** | Scythe mature crops at enemy barracks. Farms refresh on revisit.            |
| **Container**     | Break pots, barrels, crates at barracks, fortresses, and ruins.             |
| **Wild Plant**    | Only Aloe and Agave grow wild in the jungle.                                |
| **NPC Drop**      | Only Corn Seed (from Intrusion Scouts).                                     |

No merchants sell seeds in survival mode.

## Base Map Seeds

### Cotton Seed · 棉花种子

- **Cannot be ground** — must find directly
- **Barracks farm**: Western Rainforest (Flint Tribe), Mangrove Swamp
- **Containers**: Very common. Western Rainforest, Mangrove, Pit Hill, Flat Top Mountain, Frostleaf Grove. Also Ruins (Lv20–60).
- Fertilizer: Compost · Growth temp: 15–35°C

### Corn Seed · 玉米种子

- **Grinder**: 1× Corn → Corn Seed
- **Containers**: Extremely common everywhere. Also Ruins (Lv10–60).
- **NPC drop**: Intrusion Scouts (1–3)
- Fertilizer: Bone Powder · Growth temp: 10–40°C

### Pumpkin Seed · 南瓜种子

- **Grinder**: 1× Pumpkin → Pumpkin Seed
- **Containers**: Very common. Western Rainforest, Pit Hill, Flat Top Mountain, Frostleaf Grove, Great Grassland.
- Fertilizer: Ash · Growth temp: 10–40°C

### Chili Seed · 辣椒种子

- **Grinder**: 1× Chili → Chili Seed
- **Containers**: Very common. Western Rainforest, Mangrove, Pit Hill, Flat Top Mountain, Frostleaf Grove.
- Fertilizer: Stone Powder · Growth temp: 10–35°C

### Tomato Seed · 西红柿种子

- **Grinder**: 1× Tomato → Tomato Seed
- **Containers**: Less common. Western Rainforest, Fortress only.
- Not available in Shifting Sands.
- Fertilizer: Bone Powder · Growth temp: 10–35°C

### Cashew Seed · 腰果种子

- **Grinder**: 1× Cashew → Cashew Seed
- **Containers**: Fortress-level loot only (Large Tribe chests/pots).
- Fertilizer: Bone Powder · Growth temp: 15–35°C

### Peanut Seed · 花生种子

- **Grinder**: 1× Peanut → Peanut Seed
- **Containers**: Pit Hill, Flat Top Mountain, Frostleaf Grove.
- Fertilizer: Bone Powder · Growth temp: 10–40°C

### Papaya Seed · 番木瓜种子

- **Grinder**: 1× Papaya → Papaya Seed
- **Containers**: Great Grassland (Savanna) barracks only. Relatively rare.
- Fertilizer: Stone Powder · Growth temp: 10–40°C

### Guava Seed · 番石榴种子

- **Grinder**: 1× Guava → Guava Seed
- **Containers**: Great Grassland and Highlands barracks only.
- Fertilizer: Stone Powder · Growth temp: 5–40°C

### Tobacco Seed · 烟草种子

- **Cannot be ground** — must find directly
- **Barracks farm**: Pit Hill (Fang Tribe), Great Grassland
- **Containers**: Pit Hill, Great Grassland barracks chests.
- Fertilizer: Ash · Growth temp: 5–35°C

### Aloe Seed · 芦荟种子

- **Cannot be ground** — must find directly
- **Wild plants**: South of Northern Barracks, southeast of Claw Tribe, near Ancient Ruins east of Flint Barracks. Hard to spot — lower Foliage Quality in settings.
- **Barracks farm**: Pit Hill, Flat Top Mountain
- **Containers**: Clay pots at Pit Hill, Flat Top Mountain.
- Also available in DLC.
- Fertilizer: Compost · Growth temp: 0–40°C

### Agave Seed · 龙舌兰种子

- **Cannot be ground** — must find directly
- **Wild plants**: Various jungle and highland areas. Also harvestable agave trees.
- **Barracks farm**: Pit Hill (Fang Tribe)
- **Containers**: Pit Hill, Flat Top Mountain, Frostleaf Grove, Highlands.
- Also available in DLC.
- Fertilizer: Compost · Growth temp: 0–40°C

### Rubber Seed · 橡胶种子

- **Cannot be ground** — must find directly
- **Barracks farm**: Mangrove Swamp (1 seed per harvest)
- ⚠️ **Exists in game files but cannot be planted.** Rubber is obtained via the Distiller (Serum + Acidic Fluid) or looted from barracks.

- **Cannot be ground** — must find directly
- **Barracks farm**: Savage Horn Barracks (River Delta). DLC cotton replacement.
- **Containers**: Very common. DLC tribe boxes, pots, relic chests (Lv20). Break jars and barrels.

### Tips

- **Scythe barracks farms**: Use a bronze+ scythe on mature crops — yields seeds alongside the crop. Farms refresh if you leave and return.
- **Break everything**: Clay pots and wooden barrels inside barracks frequently contain seeds.
- **Monkey method**: Captured monkeys set to follow will gather wild fruits/nuts from tree stumps. Grind those into seeds.
- **Spotting wild aloe**: Lower Foliage Quality to "Low" to see aloe through dense jungle vegetation.
- **Rubber workaround**: Can't plant rubber. Get it via Distiller (Awareness Strength 54) or loot from Mangrove/Lakeside barracks.
